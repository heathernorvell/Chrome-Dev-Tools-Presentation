The console itself

The chrome console can be brought up multiple ways:

  • By right clicking on anything and hitting 'Inspect Element'
  • By hitting the hamburger menu at the top then going to Tools>Developer Tools
  • Or by hitting cmd + opt + i on mac and f12 or ctrl + shift + i on windows
